Niall (Pyrocynical) is a British Youtuber who does commentary videos and former Fortnite player. Pyrocynical joined Youtube in 2013 but didn't start uploading videos until 2014.

Niall was born on May 14, 1997 and as of December 16, 2019, He's 22.

During 2014, Niall uploaded his first video, titled "How to Pizza", an edited clip of a Breaking Bad episode and then uploaded MLG Teletubbies. Niall has repeated on how he regrets making this video, to which he refuses to make any more content similar to it. Niall hit 250,000 subscribers in 2015 and made a video thanking his viewers that got him to the milestone.
